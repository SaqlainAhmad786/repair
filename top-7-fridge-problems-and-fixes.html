Top 7 Common Fridge Problems and How to Fix Them

A refrigerator is one of the most important appliances in any home. When it breaks down, it can cause inconvenience and even spoil your food. Here are the top 7 fridge problems and how you can fix them.

1. Fridge Not Cooling Properly

  • Possible Causes: Dirty condenser coils, faulty thermostat, or low refrigerant.
  • Solution: Clean coils regularly and call a technician for gas refill or thermostat replacement.

2. Water Leaking from the Fridge

  • Possible Causes: Blocked defrost drain or cracked water supply line.
  • Solution: Unclog the drain and replace damaged water lines.

3. Unusual Noises

  • Possible Causes: Worn-out evaporator fan or compressor issues.
  • Solution: Replace faulty fan or contact a professional for compressor inspection.

4. Ice Maker Not Working

  • Possible Causes: Blocked water line or defective inlet valve.
  • Solution: Clear blockages or replace the valve.

5. Fridge Light Not Working

  • Possible Causes: Burnt-out bulb or faulty door switch.
  • Solution: Replace bulb or door switch.

6. Frost Build-Up in Freezer

  • Possible Causes: Damaged door seal or defrost system failure.
  • Solution: Replace gasket or fix the defrost timer.

7. Fridge Running Constantly

  • Possible Causes: Dirty coils or broken thermostat.
  • Solution: Clean coils and check the thermostat.

Conclusion: Regular maintenance can prevent most fridge problems. For complex repairs, always call a professional fridge repair service to avoid further damage.
