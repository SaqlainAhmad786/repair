6 Common Microwave Oven Problems and Their Solutions

Microwave ovens make cooking faster and easier, but like any appliance, they can develop issues over time. Here are the 6 most common microwave problems and what to do about them.

1. Microwave Not Heating

  • Cause: Faulty magnetron or high-voltage diode.
  • Solution: Replace faulty partsâ€”requires a professional.

2. Turntable Not Rotating

  • Cause: Damaged motor or coupler.
  • Solution: Replace motor or coupler.

3. Buttons Not Working

  • Cause: Defective control panel or touchpad.
  • Solution: Replace panel or repair circuitry.

4. Sparking Inside the Microwave

  • Cause: Damaged waveguide cover or metal inside.
  • Solution: Replace cover and avoid using metal utensils.

5. Microwave Shuts Off Midway

  • Cause: Overheating due to faulty fan.
  • Solution: Replace cooling fan.

6. Strange Noise While Running

  • Cause: Faulty motor or worn-out bearings.
  • Solution: Replace faulty components.

Conclusion: While some microwave problems have simple fixes, others require expert help. Always call a trusted microwave repair service to ensure safety and proper functioning.
