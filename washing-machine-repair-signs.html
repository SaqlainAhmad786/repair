5 Signs Your Washing Machine Needs Immediate Repair

Your washing machine works hard every week, but when it shows signs of trouble, ignoring them can lead to expensive repairs. Here are 5 signs you need washing machine repair right away.

1. Unusual Noises During Operation

  • Cause: Loose drum, worn bearings, or trapped foreign objects.
  • Fix: Remove objects and replace damaged parts.

2. Water Not Draining

  • Cause: Blocked drain hose or faulty pump.
  • Fix: Clean hose or replace pump.

3. Machine Not Spinning

  • Cause: Broken belt or motor issues.
  • Fix: Replace belt or get motor checked.

4. Leaking Water

  • Cause: Damaged door seal or cracked hoses.
  • Fix: Replace seals and hoses.

5. Bad Smell from the Drum

  • Cause: Mold and detergent residue.
  • Fix: Clean drum with vinegar and baking soda.
